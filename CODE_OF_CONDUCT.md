# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for all
contributors, regardless of experience level, gender identity, sexual orientation,
disability, personal appearance, body size, race, ethnicity, age, religion, or
nationality.

## Standards

**Positive behavior:**
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported
to the project team at **conduct@elgonrpc.xyz**. All complaints will be reviewed
and investigated.
